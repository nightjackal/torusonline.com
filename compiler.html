Below (in a male British documentory voice) is the common Torus.

I, NightJackal, am the sole developer of TORUS, but that was not always the case. There was, in the early beginning, another developer of TORUS, but he has contributeed nothing. He has contributed nothing to the diffcult development of TORUS except for one thing: the name. It is true, I did not create the name TORUS--that was my friend, but the name stuck.


My friend, previously co-developer, and I were going through names of what we should name our programming language. We ideated many names: Walibear, Boa, Viper, etc...but only one names stuck. That name being TORUS.


TORUS was created to be used by the public, and for this reason I decided it would be helpful to TORUS' users to provide all resources I used to create and develop my compiler. There were in fact, very few resources I used to create the comiler, but there were a few. Some light reading, a few youtube videos, and some good explainations were all different assests I used to learn and understand the basics of compilation, how it is done, and (thank goodness) why it is done.
